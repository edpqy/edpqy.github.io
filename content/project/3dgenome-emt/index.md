## The question
How does higher-order chromatin architecture shape gene regulation across EMT states?

## What we did
- Integrated Hi-C with epigenomic layers across EMT states  
- Examined compartment/TAD-level behaviors to understand regulatory trajectories  
- Connected topology changes to epithelial vs mesenchymal transcriptional programs

## Why it matters
Provides a blueprint for understanding how 3D genome organization constrains or enables cell-state transitions.
