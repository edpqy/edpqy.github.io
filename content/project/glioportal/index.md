## Why it matters
Glioblastoma (GBM) is highly heterogeneous and can undergo microenvironment-driven state transitions, including mesenchymal programs associated with aggressiveness and recurrence.

## What GlioPortal provides
- Gene expression exploration across tumors and patient-derived models  
- Co-expression / correlation analyses  
- Survival stratification and subgroup comparisons  
- A practical entry point for testing ligand–receptor hypotheses

## What I contributed
- Resource design and analysis flow  
- Multi-cohort integration and computational analyses  
- Translation of biological questions into interactive, reusable outputs
