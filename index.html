Professional Summary

I work at the intersection of computational biology and translational neuro-oncology, focusing on glioblastoma (GBM) heterogeneity, therapy resistance, and microenvironment-driven state transitions. My work integrates multi-omics analysis with mechanistic validation in patient-derived models, and I build resources that accelerate collaborative hypothesis testing.
